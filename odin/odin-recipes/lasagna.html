Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. The combination of these ingredients makes for a hearty, flavorful meal that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions. It takes a bit of time to prepare, but the results are totally worth it!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles (cooked according to package instructions)
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the ground beef, onion, and garlic, cooking until the beef is browned and the onions are soft.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. While the sauce is simmering, cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese and egg, mixing until smooth.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  7. Place 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce, followed by a layer of ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and another layer of meat sauce.
  8. Repeat layering the noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and meat sauce until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  9. Sprinkle the Parmesan cheese over the top layer of sauce.
  10. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  11. Let the lasagna cool for about 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
